  • Passengers must be wearing a mask at all times, and maintain 6 feet of distance. Please ensure that you have your own sanitizer at all times.
  • Check your destination country about the current covid-19 situation. Check the entry requirements for your destination and carry the needed documents respectively.

    • Ticketing

  • The minimum time to the book the tickets is one week prior to the departure.
  • Incase you face any issues in the booking process, you can contact us via the contact section.

  • The allowance is going to being the same for both international and even all domestic flights.
